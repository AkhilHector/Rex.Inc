What is Rex about ?



Oh !

Rex, is basically an eCommerce product comparision application. So the user may wish to buy a product which would be having all aspects considered like:
  • Price
  • Rating
  • Scale of Genuineness

    • That's Bad

    But, if the situation is such wherein the user must visit multiple sites in order to pin down the website where in all the criteria are met then it is is a tedious job for the user.

    That's Cool

    Hence, we wish to provide simplified results from famous eCommerce shopping websites like :
  • Flipkart
  • Snapdeal
  • Amazon(India)
